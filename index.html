Welcome!

I'm currently a grad student at Cornell University using remote sensing to learn more about the physics of glaciers and the interactions between the cryosphere and the solid Earth. Here you can find some of the things I have learned along the way, as well as some documentation for the Cryosphere And Remote Sensing Toolkit (CARST) (CARST) software developed at Cornell by Whyjay Zheng, Matthew Pritchard, Andrew Melkonian, and myself.
